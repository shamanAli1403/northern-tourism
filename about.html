Who We Are

Explore Northern Pakistan is a dedicated tourism company established in 2020 with the mission of bringing the world to the majestic peaks of Pakistan. We believe that the northern areas of Pakistan are among the most beautiful places on Earth, and we want to share that beauty with you.

Our team consists of local experts, experienced guides, and travel enthusiasts who know every corner of Hunza, Skardu, Swat, and beyond. We prioritize safety, comfort, and authentic experiences above all else.
